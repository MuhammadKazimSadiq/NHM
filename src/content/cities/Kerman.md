descriptionText: >
  Kerman is a desert city with a long history of trade,
  known for its majestic Ganjali Khan complex, ancient
  citadels, and rich carpet-weaving tradition. It serves
  as a gateway to the Lut Desert, one of the hottest
  places on Earth. The city's isolation has preserved its
  unique character, making it a gem of southeastern
  Iran.
