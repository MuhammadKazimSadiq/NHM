  Mashhad is Iran’s spiritual heart, famous for housing
  the holy shrine of Imam Ali al-Ridha (as), the 8th

  intricate tilework, and rich religious history. Beyond

  traditional Persian baths, and nearby natural
  attractions like Torqabeh. Whether you're seeking
  spiritual connection or cultural immersion, Mashhad
  delivers an unforgettable journey.
